Discipline: S02 - Ethical and Legal Responsabilities
Similarly to the DECIDE Framework, which is an evaluation guide Preece:2015, Usa-DSL follows the best practices of ethical and legal issues to protect the user data, dignity, anonymity and well-being. Furthermore, it has to include some description to inform the users that they can stop the evaluation at any time they are not comfortable with some aspects of the evaluation process. At the end of this step, all the signed documents from the subjects are organized.
