Discipline: S06 - Metrics
The metrics used on USA-DSL were defined from an SLR mapping Rodrigues:2017. The metrics are comprehension/learning, ease of use, effort/conclusion time, observed complexity and efficiency. These metrics will guide the definition of the evaluation instruments questions to be applied during the evaluation. Similarly to Step 3, this step has only one activity performed during the Planning phase.
