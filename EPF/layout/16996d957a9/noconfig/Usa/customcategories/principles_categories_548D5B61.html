Main Description

The Usa-DSL Process was designed based on the following principles:

Assist in the construction of the usability evaluation in a pratical and efficient way.

Assist in the DSL developement through continuos feedback, promoting to continuos improvement of the language.

Focus on the user-centered evaluation through all the construction phses, minimizing the DSL's rework and disuse.

Make DSLs evaluation easier and productive, aiming to reach usability criteria and usage satisfaction
